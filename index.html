Our Story

Baked in Lahore,
Loved Across Pakistan

Ezmush Bakers was founded in 2010 in the heart of Lahore with one simple belief — that great baking is an act of love. What started as a small neighbourhood bakery has grown into one of Pakistan's most trusted names in artisan confectionery.

Every product we create uses only the finest local and imported ingredients. Our master bakers bring decades of experience to every recipe, whether it's a towering wedding cake or a humble loaf of sourdough.

  • 100% fresh, baked daily — no frozen products
  • No artificial preservatives or artificial colours
  • Halal certified and hygienic preparation
  • Custom orders accepted for all occasions
